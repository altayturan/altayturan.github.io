Keep Quite Until Death

Summary

Keep Quiet Until Death is a sound based survival game.

Game takes place at a alternative earth that is currently at post-apocalyptic era.
The Iron Claws did take over the world and looking for more human flesh to grow even more. As a player you wake up in a bunker and find a note from professor who thinks that he can save the earth. So you start an advanture to find the professor.



Game

There is creatures named "Iron Claw".
Iron Claws can't see but they can hear and they are after your flesh. You must use your sling to keep them distracted and away from you.
You also have a gun but not much ammo. Be carefull though, even tho you kill a Iron Claw with one shot many others will hear it.

Also we have loot mechanic.
Player can loot scrap and will be able to craft in the future updates. Player can also loot notes from storyline and bullets to kill more Iron Claws.
